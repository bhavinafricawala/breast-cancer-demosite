Donate Clothing and Household Goods

Donate your new or gently used clothing, shoes, and small household items to United Breast Cancer Research Foundation to help fight breast cancer. Your donations are given to local thrift stores, and the proceeds are used to support breast cancer research.

Your Donation Is Tax-Deductible

Then your donation is picked up or dropped off at one of our community donation centers you will receive a donation receipt. Any single item that's valued over $500 must be accompanied by a certified appraisal to be claimed on your tax return. (For more information on valuing and claiming donations, please visit irs.gov. United Breast Cancer Research Foundation does not offer any appraisal services.

Schedule a Donation Pickup

Do you live in the Central Valley, Sacramento area or the San Francisco Bay area? You can schedule a pickup of your donated items. We visit most communities in the area at least once every four to six weeks. To find out when we'll be in your neighborhood next, use our convenient online scheduling form or call 1-800-788-6444.
